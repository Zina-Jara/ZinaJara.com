About me

A small introduction about my self

Zina Jara

Actress
From Greece Residing in Toronto

Hello and thank you for stopping by! I’m Zina Jara, an actor, filmmaker, and storyteller who has been immersed in the world of theater and performance since the age of six. From the moment I first stepped on stage, I knew this was where I belonged. Acting is where I get to explore human nature, the way I connect with the world, and bring stories to life in ways that move and inspire audiences. With a background in drama and media production, I’ve had the opportunity to work in theater, film, and live performance in Toronto, Montreal, and Greece. Whether performing, directing, or creating behind the scenes, I am always searching for truth in storytelling—moments that feel real, raw, and that leave a lasting impression. This portfolio is a window into my journey, my experiences, the characters I’ve had the privilege of bringig to life and the roles that have shaped me. Feel free to explore, and if you’re looking for someone who brings depth, dedication, creativity, authenticity and a little bit of magic to every project, let’s connect.Looking forward to future endeavors.
ZINA JARA.
